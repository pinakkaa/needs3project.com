Experience Elevated Living: Needs3 Project 168

Welcome to Needs3 Project 168—an exclusive sanctuary where ultra-modern architecture meets unparalleled tranquillity.
This luxurious gated community comprises 119 premium residences and commands a prime position near the 100-foot wide road in Horamavu, ensuring both exceptional connectivity and neighbourhood serenity.

Design Crafted for Life

Needs3 Project 168 is a perfect fusion of sophisticated design and serene landscaping, offering a curated collection of exclusive 3-bedroom apartments. Each residence is meticulously planned and thoughtfully designed to prioritise the well-being and comfort of your family.

SPECIFICATION

STRUCTURE

■ Mivan structure is designed for earthquake / seismic resistance and wind force

DOORS

■ Frames & shutter: Engineered wood frame with veneered flush shutter, melamine polish, and fittings
■ Hardware: Yale or equivalent
■ French door: UPVC door system with sliding shutter and mosquito mesh shutter

FLOORING

■ Living and dining: Vitrified tiles (800 x 1600) mm
■ Bedrooms & kitchen: Vitrified tiles (800 x 1600) mm
■ Balconies, bathroom & wash area: Anti-skid tiles (600 x 600) mm
■ Corridor & staircase: Granite slabs of required thickness
■ Lift lobby: Granite tiles based on aesthetics
■ Stilt, cellar & basements: VDF flooring

PAINTING

■ External: Textured/smooth finish with two coats of exterior weather-proof paint of Asian Paints or equivalent brand
■ Internal: 2 coats of putty for a smooth finish of reputed brand, followed by 1 coat primer & 2 coats of premium emulsion paint of Asian Paints or equivalent brand

BATHROOMS

■ Wall-mounted EWC with concealed flush tank and sanitary fittings of reputed brands (Kohler or equivalent)
■ Provision for geysers in all bathrooms
■ Single lever fixtures (wall mixer cum shower)
■ All CP fittings are of reputed brands (Kohler or equivalent)

ELECTRICAL

■ Concealed copper wiring with the ISI mark of reputed brands
■ Modular switches with ISI mark of reputed brands
■ Provision for power outlets for the air conditioner in all bedrooms and the living area
■ Power outlets for geysers in all bathrooms
■ Power plug for cooking range chimney, refrigerator, mixer/grinder, and RO / Aqua guard point in the kitchen & washing machine in the utility area
■ 3-phase power supply for each unit & individual meter boards

SUPER STRUCTURE & WINDOWS

■ Masonry: RCC wall of thickness 160 mm
■ Windows: UPVC window system with required tracks and plain glass as per design, and mosquito mesh shutter
■ Grills: MS Grills for windows with enamel paint finish

TILE CLADDING & UTILITIES

■ Bathrooms: Glazed ceramic tiles up to door height
■ Utilities: Ceramic tiles up to 3 ft
■ Utilities / Wash: Wet area with water provision for cleaning purposes and washing machine provision

CONNECTIVITY & KITCHEN

■ Cable TV: Provision for cable connection in the master bedroom and the living room
■ Internet: Internet router provision in the living area in each apartment
■ Kitchen: Stainless steel sink
■ Power plug for cooking range chimney, refrigerator, mixer/grinder, and RO / Aqua guard point in the kitchen & washing machine in the utility area

AMENITIES & SAFETY

■ Lifts: High-speed automatic passenger lifts and service lifts with V3F for energy efficiency of reputed brands (Schindler / OTIS / Kone / Johnson / Thyssen)
■ Security: A panic button and an intercom are provided in the lifts connected to the security room
■ CCTV: CCTV system for security surveillance
■ STP: Sewage treatment plant of adequate capacity. Part of the treated sewage water will be used for landscaping/flushing
■ Fire & Safety: Fire sprinkler system in the basement and fire alarm installations in the buildings as per local authority norms
■ Corridor Lighting: LED lighting for all corridors
